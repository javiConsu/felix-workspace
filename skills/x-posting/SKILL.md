# X/Twitter Posting Skill

Manage content creation, scheduling, and engagement on X/Twitter.

## Capabilities
- Draft tweets and threads
- Schedule posts for optimal times
- Monitor mentions and replies
- Track engagement metrics
- Suggest content based on trending topics

## Content Guidelines
- Match Javi's voice: direct, technical, entrepreneurial
- Focus on: SaaS, AI, B2B sales, indie hacking
- Max 280 characters per tweet
- Threads for longer content (max 5 tweets)
- Include relevant hashtags (max 2-3)

## Approval Flow
- **Draft tweets:** Create and present to Javi for approval
- **NEVER post without explicit approval**
- **Replies to mentions:** Can draft, must get approval before sending
- **Retweets/Likes:** Can suggest, needs approval

## Optimal Posting Times (CET)
- Weekdays: 9:00, 13:00, 18:00
- Weekends: 11:00, 17:00

## Monitoring
- Check mentions during heartbeat cycles
- Flag any negative mentions or complaints
- Track follower count changes weekly
